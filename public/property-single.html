Property Description

Situated in the heart of Bangalore, Litestore-Indiranagar offers the perfect blend of a commercial location without the traffic and noise of bustling roads

Our LiteStore Flagship Space has a welcoming frontage with an inviting window display, the venue is filled with a bright natural light. The interiors are tasteful with wooden floors, giving the venue a dynamic feel. It is a blank canvas ideal for creative professionals and DesignHub to launch their ideal temporary storefront. It is located in the high sought out area of 80 ft road & surrounded by many popular restaurants ensuring a high footfall throughout.
