Frequently Asked Questions

LiteStore™ Services

  • What are LiteStore™ Services?

    They are add on services that you can avail for setting up your pop-up store. Our goal is to make your journey of opening a pop-up store easier, efficient and transparent. So we do all the heavy lifting! While you can concentrateon what you do best!

    We provide:

    ● PoS & other payment systems
    ● Retail Store design:
    ● Fit-outs: including signage, rental soft furnishing.
    ● Staffing: including maintenance & security
    ● Wifi

    All the above services will be tailor-made for your brand and your marketing strategy.

  • How do I book LiteStore™ services?

    All LiteStore™ services are listed on the website and our essentials can be booked on our site when you book the space.

  • How much will I be charged for the LiteStore™ Services?

    You will be charged for the services you subscribe for. Think of it like an amazon cart! However, we adopt a staggered/tiered pricing model. The more services you opt for and the longer the duration you engage our services,the cheaper the cost.

  • What if I face any problem with LiteStore™ services?

    Please reach out to us at support@litestore.in. Our team will get in touch with you.

  • I'm a service provider and would like to get involved as a partner. How do I get in touch?

    We are always on the lookout for partners who can help us create the best stores for our clients. Please reach out to us at support@litestore.in

  • What can I expect from the space that I see on LiteStore™.in?

    Our photography will ensure that you get an accurate idea of the space on our site. Further, each of the listed spaces will go through a round of due diligence from our side to ensure that basic amenities such as electricity,water supply & maintenance will be provided. The details on the property page will provide you with information specific to each booking.

  • Who takes care of the fittings/furnishing/lighting?

    Each store will be provided in a white label manner with basic lighting and fittings. You can choose LiteStore™ Services for anything additional.

  • Can I make alterations to the space?

    Spaces are booked "as seen". This means that you agree to book the space in the condition that it is shown in the listing. For most spaces landlords are happy for you to redecorate as long as the space is returned to itsoriginal condition at the end of your booking. If there is anything you are unsure about in regards to the decoration, make sure you check with the landlord first or get in touch with our team and we'll look into itfor you.

  • Can I share the space with any other brand/retailer?

    Yes, LiteStore™ offers the option to book the space in collaboration with other brands to improve the offering at your store and build synergies with other businesses.

Covid-19 Information

  • What Covid-19 Protocols will be followed?

    We follow all the Covid-19 protocols mandated by the World Health Organization and the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. This includes mask wearing, ensuring physical distancing, and controlling the number of employeesand customers in stores, improving speed of service, and introducing more self-service options.

Designers

  • What's the LiteStore™ DesignHub 2021?

    We believe that the future of retail belongs to stores which can narrate stories, create experiences and bring out innovative concepts. At the DesignHub 2021, we offer you an exciting opportunity to showcase your best interiordesigns & store concepts and get an opportunity to bring them to life.

  • How does it work?

    You design a pop-up store on an empty canvas provided by us and upload the designed stores on our website.

    We help you connect with brands to design and launch their stores.

  • Who owns the design/concept I submit on LiteStore™.in?

    The copyright of your design and the concept completely rests with you. We will be listing your design concepts only to prospective brands. These brands would have agreed to the Terms of Use agreement, which specificallyprohibits them from infringing on your copyright.

  • What do I keep in mind before designing a store?

    Know that you are designing temporary spaces for brands. The duration could range from a few weeks to a few months. Therefore, the design element must be aesthetically minimalist while ensuring that no structural changescan be made on the property. Further, the cost must be kept minimal since these are temporary spaces.

  • What other details should I mention along with uploading my store design?

    You will have to mention the following details:

    ● The number of days it will take to implement the idea:please ensure that the design can be brought to life within 2-3 days
    ● The availability of the soft furniture:
    ● Rough estimate of your charges:

  • Can I upload more than one design/concept?

    Yes. We encourage you to apply as many designs/concepts on your portfolio. It will allow brands to explore more of your work and increase your visibility.

  • How do I design a store when I do not know the specifications of the brand?

    The aim of the DesignHub 2021 is to bring out as many interesting concepts to the forefront in the form of design templates that could be applicable to many brands without having regard to the exact nature of the brand.

    However, once the Brand likes any of your design/concepts, it will reach out to you listing out their specifications.

  • What else should be mentioned on my portfolio?

    Apart from uploading the design/concepts, you would have to mention the preference of stores you would want to work on along with any limitations. And the charges

  • How do I get in touch with brands/retailers?

    The brands/retailers once they register and confirm their booking with us, will be introduced to all your design concepts on our website. Based on their interest, they will connect with you.

  • What happens once a brand is interested in my store design and they approach me?

    You will be put in touch with our LiteStore™ Furnishing team. You are then required to collaborate with our team and bring the store to life.

  • How will I be paid for my services?

    The Brands will make payments to LiteStore™ and the payment will be credited to the Designer within five business days in accordance with the Designer Terms and Conditions.
