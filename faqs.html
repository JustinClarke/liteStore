Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs for Landowners

  • Who are you entering into a contract with?

    If you are listing your space on the Litestore Platform, you will enter into a contract with each Brand that makes a booking. This will include the Usage Terms determined at the time of making the booking.
    If you own a space that has been leased and operated by Litestore, you will enter into a lease agreement with Litestore through its registered entity.

  • Will your space be safe from squatters? What if a brand refuses to vacate the space?

    When a brand makes a booking on the Litestore platform, they are not entitled to legal possession of the property. They only enjoy a limited license to use the property. If a brand exceeds the checkout time, they will be liable to pay a penalty to the Landowner. Litestore will undertake all expenses related to ensuring that Brands check out of the space on time.

FAQs for Brands

  • How safe is my booking with Litestore?

    Litestore ensures that booking and launching a store is a seamless experience. If for any reason, you are unable to launch your store due to any fault committed by the landowner or Litestore, your booking amount will be refunded in accordance with the Refund Policy.

  • Who are you entering into a contract with?

    If you book a Litestore Flagship Space, you will enter into an agreement directly with Litestore at the time you agree to our Terms of Use/License Terms. Every time you complete a booking and Usage Terms are agreed upon, there is a concluded agreement between you and Litestore. If you book a Listed Space on the Litestore Platform, you will enter into an agreement with each Landowner at the time you make a booking and the Usage Terms are agreed to by the Landowner. The Landowner and the Brand will be bound by the Litestore Terms of Use/License Agreement along with the Usage Terms of each booking.

  • Will you have to bring your own staff/ equipment?

    Litestore offers Litestore Services which every brand can utilise by booking the same on our platform. These services are optional and will allow you to launch your store with the least hassle.

  • What expenses will a Brand have to incur, over and above the booking charge?

    The booking charge is inclusive of all costs involved in opening the store. Any operational costs incurred while the store is open which are not covered in the Terms of Use/License Agreement will be borne by the brand.

  • Can I book for a weekend only?

    Yes, in some cases you can book for weekends only. You can find out the minimum booking period for a space by checking the space listing.

  • Who takes care of the fittings/furnishing/lighting?

    Each store will be provided in a white label manner with basic lighting and fittings. You can choose LiteStore Services for anything more and anything specific.

  • Can I make alterations to the space?

    Spaces are rented "as seen". This means that you agree to rent the space in the condition that it is shown in the space listing or shown on the viewing. For most spaces landlords are happy for you to redecorate as long as the space is returned to its original condition at the end of your booking. If there is anything you are unsure about in regards to the decoration, make sure you check with the landlord first or get in touch with our team and we'll look into it for you.
